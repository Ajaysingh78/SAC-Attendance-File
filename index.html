What is SAC Attendance System?

The SAC Attendance System is a QR-based smart attendance portal developed to ensure:

  • Location-restricted submissions
  • Device-lock mechanism
  • Security against misuse

📌 Who Can Use This System?

  • All registered students of SAC.
  • Only students whose emails are registered in the system can successfully submit attendance.
  • First-time users must scan their QR code using this website.

🛠️ Requirements Before Use

  • A valid QR code generated specifically for you.
  • Enable location permission in your browser.
  • Use your personal device only.
  • Don't use any third-party QR scanner app.

🚀 How to Submit Attendance (Step-by-Step)

Step 1️ – Open Website

Visit: Click here

Step 2️ – Click “Start Scanner”

Step 3️ – Allow Location Access

Step 4️ – Scan Your QR Code

  • ✅ You are inside the campus.
  • ✅ You are using your registered device.

Step 5️ – Form Opens Automatically

Click "Submit" – your attendance is now recorded.

🔐 Important Security Features

  • No Proxy: Device-lock ensures one student cannot use another’s QR.
  • Location-Based Access: Form opens only inside campus boundaries.
  • One-Time Device Registration: Others can't misuse your QR.
